    Who we are?


    At New Gen Patio, we are more than just builders of beautiful and durable covered patios and pergolas; we are passionate creators of unique outdoor spaces where you can savor family dinners and forge lasting memories with friends. Owned by Rafael Cuza and Alejandro Alonso, our vision is to enhance your home and enrich your life by creating inviting environments that foster connection and joy.

    Every project begins with your vision, and we pride ourselves on transforming your imagination into reality. As leaders in the Texas aluminum construction sector, our mission is to provide exceptional customer service, ensuring that your needs and expectations are always our top priority. We believe that effective communication is the key to a successful partnership, and we strive to maintain transparency throughout the entire process—from the initial consultation to project completion.

    Safety is our utmost priority; we ensure that every structure adheres to the highest standards, granting you peace of mind as you enjoy your new space. We also pay meticulous attention to design, recognizing that each project is a personal reflection of your desires. With a collaborative spirit, we work closely with you to ensure the final result not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

    At New Gen Patio, we are your partners in crafting stunning, safe, and functional outdoor environments that enrich your life and create unforgettable moments. Together, let’s build the outdoor space you’ve always dreamed of.
