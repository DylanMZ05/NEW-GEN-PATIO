    How do your pergolas differ from wooden pergolas? ▶

    Our Aluminum cover patios offer several advantages over wooden patios. First, they are more durable and resistant to weather elements, meaning they won't warp, crack, or rot over time. Second, aluminum requires minimal maintenance, eliminating the need for regular staining or sealing. Additionally, aluminum patios have superior fire resistance, making them a safer option for outdoor living spaces. Lastly, they provide superior energy efficiency, as they reflect heat and help keep outdoor spaces cooler, making them a practical and long-lasting choice for homeowners.

    What is the process from start to finish? ▶

    Step 1: Request an Online Quote Get started by requesting a free online quote or roughly number by phone call to understand the estimated cost of your project. This allows you to see if we align with your budget before moving forward.

    Step 2: Schedule an In-Home Consultation If the budget looks good, we’ll arrange an in-home consultation to view your space, discuss the project in detail, ensure that we help you with some design ideas and bring to live your vision.

    Step 3: Receive a Formal Quote and Visualization of the Project. Following our visit, we’ll provide a comprehensive final bid, including a 3D rendering, architectural plans, and the full project scope—giving you a vivid preview of your new space.

    Step 4: Sign Building Contract and Deposit. If you like the final project proposal, we’ll prepare a contract detailing the project, collect deposit (just 25% of Project Value), and any other documentation that you may required. (References, Full Covered Insurance and others

    Step 5: Schedule Your Construction Start Date Once all the paperwork is complete, we’ll set a start date for your project, and you can embrace the excitement of seeing your ideas come to life!

    Step 6: Regular Updates Previous the Construction Once scheduled on our construction calendar, we will notify you in the weeks leading up to the start date to keep you informed of the exact date, as it may change due to weather conditions, but we will always keep you updated.

    Step 7: Show Time!. As your project gets underway, we understand the importance of meeting your needs and addressing any questions you may have throughout the construction process. That’s why our entire team will be here to make sure you and your family feel comfortable and confident every step of the way. We’re committed to delivering results that exceed your expectations.

    Step 8: Final Walk-Through and Review After completion, we’ll do a final walk-through or follow-up call to confirm everything meets your expectations. If you’re pleased with the outcome, we’d greatly appreciate a positive review—it’s the best compliment we can receive!

    What materials do you use for the roofing? ▶

    Our roofs are crafted from high-quality insulated roofing panels, featuring two layers of powder-coated aluminum with a 3-inch core of compressed styrofoam. These panels not only deliver exceptional thermal insulation to keep your space cooler and more comfortable, but they also boast impressive durability against the elements. The powder coating provides long-lasting protection against rust, corrosion, and fading, ensuring your investment stands the test of time. Additionally, we offer options with polycarbonate or metracrylate for projects that require it, allowing natural light to brighten your space while still protecting against harmful solar rays and ensuring lasting durability

    Cost per square feet? ▶

    We don't provide pricing based on square footage because our products are custom-made to fit each client’s unique needs. For very small projects, our starting price is $4,500. However, the final cost will depend on various factors, including the size of the project, design complexity, materials used, and any additional features you may want. This approach allows us to deliver tailored solutions that ensure you receive the best quality and value for your investment

    What warranty do you offer ▶

    At New Gen Patio, we offer a 5-year warranty on all our projects because we believe in the quality and durability of our work. This warranty covers both labor and materials, so you can trust that your investment is protected well beyond project completion. If any issues related to construction or materials arise during this period, we’ll be there to address them promptly. We are committed to delivering exceptional quality and ensuring your complete satisfaction with your outdoor space, so you can enjoy it worry-free for years to come."

    What about storms? ▶

    At New Gen Patio, safety is one of our top priorities. Our patios are engineered to withstand an average wind resistance of 140 mph, with the capability to hold up to 170 mph, while guaranteeing a minimum of 120 mph by contract-equivalent to Hurricane T3 winds. Before starting any project, we always inspect the area where we will install your patio or pergola to ensure optimal conditions. We achieve durability by securely anchoring our structures to concrete using robust systems and building a strong inner foundation for enhanced stability. Each project undergoes thorough engineering calculations to ensure we meet or exceed wind resistance standards. This commitment ensures your outdoor space is not only beautiful but also resilient against the elements."
